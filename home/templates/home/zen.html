Our Zen

What inspires us to deliver a great product.

Risk management should be participative
All employees who are somehow involved in identifying and managing risks across the organisation should have a say in the process.
Communication is everything
We promote the interaction between individuals across hierarchy and locations as the best way to drive risk awareness and trigger actions.
Users need flexibility
We prefer to give users while maximum freedom to use their own words to describe the risks, controls and activities rather than picking from a one-size-fits-all risk universe. We are a Galaxy after all...
Keep it ultra simple
We take very seriously our commitment to keep complexity to a minimum. We refuse to match the 'sofistication' of other products in the market that turn risk management into a tick-and-bash bureaucratic burden.
Users should say Wow!
We are committed to making Risk Management be cool, as opposed to boring, by making our software intuitive, super fast and visually stunning.
