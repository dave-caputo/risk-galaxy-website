Our product

We provide an online solution for Enterprise Risk Management (ERM) that allows organisations to register, assess, monitor and report the risks across locations and hierarchy levels. The objective is to provide the Management, the Board, Shareholders and others with a clear picture of the exposure of existing risks and how they are managed.

Why choose us

Because our solution is especifically designed to save time, money by reducing unnecessary admin burden, while meeting high governance standards expected by the Board of Directors, auditors and shareholders.

Risk Galaxy has been created to help busy employees. Those who already have a large number of day to day responsibilities, and can't allow to waste precious time in bureaucracy.
